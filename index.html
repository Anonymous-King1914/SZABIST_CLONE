About Our University

  • Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science and Technology (SKLearn Academy) is a fully Chartered Institute established through a Legislative Act of the Pakistan Assembly (Sindh Act No. XI of 1995). SKLearn Academy is approved and recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan as a degree awarding institution.
  • Today SKLearn Academy is known as a major university comprising of five campuses (including one foreign campus),three diploma centers, three intermediate colleges and one research center. More than 6500 degrees have been awarded by SKLearn Academy to date.
  • Read More

  • SKLearn Academy remains the first choice for students coming from socio-economically diverse groups of the society. By ensuring the best standards of educational process, SKLearn Academy remains one of the best educational institutions as we make distinctive contributions to society in research, learning and teaching. We provide quality education at affordable cost which speaks about our values and gives us an edge over other educational institutions in the country.

  • Khusro Pervaiz Khan / Head of Campus
